Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

MediaKit Creator ("we", "us", or "our") operates a dashboard that allows TikTok content creators to display their analytics and media kit to potential brand partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information only after explicit authorization via TikTok OAuth 2.0:

  • TikTok profile information (display name, avatar, bio)
  • Public account statistics (follower count, total likes, video count)
  • Video metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) for recent posts

We do not collect passwords, private messages, or any non-public TikTok data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Display a read-only media kit page for the authenticated creator
  • Share creator metrics with potential brand partners upon the creator's request

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only displayed on your personal media kit page, which you control.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored only while your account is active. You may revoke access at any time through TikTok's app permissions settings, which will delete your data from our systems within 30 days.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of access tokens and HTTPS communication.

7. Contact
